3-Ingredient Nutella Pretzel Milkshake

The easiest & best milkshake recipe upgrade ever.

I do believe that Nutella and pretzels may be one of the best combos ever. Just saying.

This indulgent Nutella pretzel shake is made with 3 ingredients and takes just 4 minutes to make. This is a perfect refreshing and easy treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (360g) vanilla ice cream
  • Â¼ cup (70g) Nutella, plus more for garnish
  • 2 pretzel rods, 1 in the milkshake, 1 for garnish

Method

  1. In a blender, combine the ice cream, one pretzel rod, and your Nutella and blend. Start on low until it begins to come together and then increase the speed to high until combined.
  2. Pour the milkshake into a glass and dip your other pretzel rod into your container of Nutella and place it, Nutella side down, in your milkshake for a nice surprise. Serve and enjoy.
